## How to Reorder Cards

### Drag-and-Drop Method
1. **Long-press a card** (hold for approximately 1 second)
2. **Drag the card** up or down to your desired position
3. **Release** to drop the card in place
4. The card slides into its new position, and all other cards automatically renumber

### Multi-Select Cut and Paste Method
1. **Long-press a card** (hold for approximately 1 second) to enter multi-select mode
2. **Tap additional cards** to add them to your selection
3. **Long-press the card** after which you want to move your selected cards
4. The multi-select action menu appears
5. **Tap "Paste"**
6. Selected cards are moved to the new location

For more details, see [Multi-Select Operations](/docs/cards-list/multi-select/).
