## How to Delete Cards in the cards list
**From Cards List:**

### Delete Single Card

#### Method 1: Swipe a card left or right to delete single card
**Fastest method** for single card deletion.

#### Method 2: Card Menu to delete single card
1. **Tap a card** to open card action menu
2. **Tap "Delete"**

### Delete Multiple Cards

**Use Multi-Select:**

2. **Tap additional cards** to select them
3. **Tap "Delete" action** (in selection menu)
4. **Confirm bulk deletion**
5. **All selected cards removed**
