Multi-select mode allows you to perform bulk operations on multiple cards simultaneously, making it easy to reorganize decks, reposition cards, or delete groups of cards at once.

## Entering Multi-Select Mode
Multi-select is only available in the card list.

#### Method 1: From Card Menu
1. **Tap a card** to open the card action menu
2. **Select "Select / Cut"** from the menu

## Selecting Additional Cards

Once in multi-select mode:
- **Tap any card** to add it to your selection
- the card changes color to indicate selection
- **Tap again** to deselect individual cards
