## Card Actions

### Tap a Card

**Tap a Card** opens the card's **action menu:**

- **Edit** - Modify term/definition (see [Editing Cards](/docs/cards-list/editing/))
- **Delete** - Remove this card
- **Select / Cut** - Enter multi-select mode (see [Multi-Select](/docs/cards-list/multi-select/))
- **Add a new card here** - Insert a new card after this position

### Long-Press a Card

**Long-Press a Card** select the card and switch to the multi-select mode.
