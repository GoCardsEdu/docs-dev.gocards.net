<center><img src="/images/docs/create-deck.png" width="300" /></center>

Organize related flashcards into decks.

1. **Tap the "+" button** (top-right, available in Recent Decks or All Decks tabs)
2. **Enter deck name** (e.g., "Spanish Verbs", "Biology Chapter 3")
3. **Tap "Create"**

The deck appears in your All Decks list and will show in Recent Decks once you use it.
